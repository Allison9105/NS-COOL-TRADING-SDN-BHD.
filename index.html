🏢 公司简介 🏢 Company Profile 🏢 Profil Syarikat

NS Cool Trading Sdn Bhd 位于新山 Taman Johor Jaya，是一家专业销售汽车冷气（冷却系统）零件的公司。我们提供各类品牌及车型的冷气系统零件，从压缩机、蒸发器到冷凝器，一应俱全。

NS Cool Trading Sdn Bhd, based in Taman Johor Jaya, Johor Bahru, specialises in retail sales of car air-conditioning parts. We stock a wide range of components for all major brands and vehicle models — from compressors and evaporators to condensers and more.

压缩机 Compressor Compressor
Compressor / 压缩机

冷气系统核心零件，适配多款国产及进口车型。 Core component of the AC system, compatible with most local and imported vehicles. Komponen utama sistem aircond, sesuai untuk pelbagai model kereta.

冷却盘管 Cooling Coil Cooling Coil
Cooling Coil / 冷却盘管

负责车内制冷，确保冷气效果最佳。 Responsible for cabin cooling — ensures peak AC performance. Bertanggungjawab menyejukkan kabin kenderaan anda.

冷凝器 Condenser Condenser
Condenser / 冷凝器

散热关键零件，维持整个系统稳定运作。 Key heat-dissipation part, keeps the entire AC system stable. Komponen penyejukan haba penting untuk kestabilan sistem.

💨
风机马达 Blower Motor Blower Motor
Blower Motor / 风机马达

控制冷气风量，确保车内空气循环顺畅。 Controls airflow and ensures smooth air circulation inside the cabin. Mengawal aliran udara dan memastikan pengedaran udara yang lancar.

🔩
干燥罐 Receiver Drier Receiver Drier
Receiver Drier / 干燥罐

过滤冷媒中的水分与杂质，保护系统零件。 Filters moisture and impurities from refrigerant to protect the system. Menapis kelembapan dan kekotoran daripada refrigeran.

Expansion Valve / 膨胀阀
